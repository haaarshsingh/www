Large Projects

AwesomeSource

While finding open-source projects to contribute to, people have to go through the struggle of finding something which suits them. The people who maintain the project, or the project owner(s), have to go through the struggle of increasing the popularity of their project to try and get folks to find out and contribute! AwesomeSource solves all of that. AwesomeSource is a website where people can find and post open-source projects, and it simplifies the process of contributing to open-source even more!

SnipBin

SnipBin is a elegant, minimalist, and blazing fast code pasting tool which was built as an alternative to PasteBin. There aren't many great ways to share code snippets in today's world. I wanted to build a tool which was simple, wasn't cluttered with bad UI or ads, and easy to use. That's why I made SnipBin. SnipBin has a simplified design, no need for a thousand things like just to share a code snippet, SnipBin keeps everything simple and to the point. It has a simple and dark UI which is comfy for the eyes to look at, and syntax highlighting for over 80 languages, all built-in.

SketchMessage

Unlike boring messaging apps, SketchMessage changes the game. It is an app where you can communicate with people all over the world by drawing on a whiteboard. This is an elegant, unique and cool project made over a week with the intention of making talking with other humans over screens less boring.

FastBot

FastBot is a highly customizable, open-source Discord Bot with loads of fun and useful commands. There are many Discord Bots out there, but this bot was made with more of a personal approach on giving memebers a better experience in the server. It has commands for fun, jokes and maintaining/moderating your Discord servers. With a total of over 40 cool and unique commands.

My first ever project with Python. Numifier is a small command line tool which turns regular text, like this, into numbers, 11k3 7h15. This gave me a very basic overview of Python's syntax, and in the process I made something simple, but very fun and cool.

Birthday Celebration

Birthday Celebration is a fun and cool website which you can use to wish others Happy Birthday in a cool and unique style. I made this for my Mom, on her Birthday, though it can be used to wish a Birthday to anyone.

Comment of Code

Comment of code is a small project where I challenged myself to write a comment in every programming language. I learnt lots of cool, weird and wacky things about how comments are written in certain languages, and was overall a fun challenge to try!

Games

All about the Jelly

All about the Jelly is an experimental and customizable game about playing around with squishy blobs of Jello where I tried to simulate the satisfying feeling of playing with squishy, jiggly Jelly.

Elanchau

Elanchau is a crazy, fast-paced 2D action game made for the GMTK game jam. The theme of the jam was "out of control", and in this game, you try shoot the enemies with your bullets flinging and bouncing all over the place, while trying to not fall down due to your extremely fast speed! Overall, it is a fun and crazy game which I had a good time making.

The Basket

The Basket is a game made for the Extra Credits game jam, where the theme was "Take Care", with an artstyle inspired from Kurzgesagt. This game, there was an invasion of 120 total birds all going for a basket full of food. You, the protector of the basket has to drive away as many of these cute, squishy birds as possible.
